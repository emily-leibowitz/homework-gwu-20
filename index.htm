Research 02

Interview Plan

The goal of the research questions is to discover methods people use to book travel. I want to understand the user’s planning process when it comes to booking travel, the tools they use to plan trips, and the pain points they experience during the planning process.

Objective: Planning Travel

Question: How often do you travel?
Question: How far in advance do you begin planning a trip?
Question: How do you start the planning process?
Question: How many people do you usually travel with?
Question: What is your planning style? Do you take your time or plan quickly?

Objective: Booking Travel

Question: How do you usually book transportation and lodging?
Question: If booking online, which device do you typically use (mobile, tablet, desktop)?
Question: Do you use rewards programs such as hotel points, frequent flyer miles, credit care rewards?
Question: About how long does it typically take you to book a trip?
Question: How do you store your reservation info?
Question: What difficulties have you experienced while booking a trip?

Objective: Communication

Question: How do you communicate with travel partners during the planning process?
Question: Do you share links and articles with others?
Question: How do you share booking details and itinerary with travel companions?
Question: How do you keep in touch during the trip?

Objective: The Perfect Travel App

Question: If there was an app that solved all your travel issues, what would that feel like? What would it do for you?

Definition and Ideation 03

Definition and Synthesis

Travel apps were designed to help users book travel. However, the majority do not alleviate the stress and time it takes to plan a trip, leaving travelers overwhelmed and using up valuable time. How might we ensure a successful travel experience that saves travelers time and money, increases trip satisfaction, and enables easy communication with peers?

Feature Prioritization
Value Proposition

As a UX Designer, I am developing a traveling app to help busy travel lovers overcome the stress and time associated with planning and booking travel. This app is elevated in its usability, efficiency, and visual appeal. Unlike the competitors, Trippi prioritizes simplicity, organization, and connection. The volume of exciting features and content is balanced with simplified interface and clear intention. Trippi is backed by months of research and planning. I believe in a higher standard when it comes to the experience of users. Intuitive displays and beautiful interface come together to create the optimal experience.

The home screen gives users a jumping off point. It highlights key features and provides a quick booking tool at the top.

Final Thoughts 05

What I learned

There are dozens of travel apps on the market, but few if any relieve the pain points many travelers face: planning is overwhelming and takes a lot of time. Trippi is dedicated to expediting the process of planning and booking travel, so it is as quick and painless as possible. Get booking, planning, organizing, and group communication done all in one place, without the hassle of multiple apps.
Get it done and get there, with Trippi.

Future Opportunities

There is a large opportunity for growth with Trippi. An ever growing catalog of information and destinations, potential for partnerships with travel brands, hotels, and airlines, the addition and revision of features and tools, and growth in the travel companion market.
